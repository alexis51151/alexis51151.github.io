About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science of Rice University advised by Konstantinos Mamouras. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from Institut Polytechnique de Paris and was a student of Telecom SudParis Engineering School.

Research Interests

My research aims to provide better abstraction and implementation of systems for data processing and IoT applications. I am currently working on more efficient streaming algorithms for pattern matching that can run on ASIC hardware. I am also interested in temporal logic for cyber-physical systems verification.
