I am a fifth-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science of Rice University advised by Prof. Konstantinos Mamouras (starting from Fall 2021).

I received my Master’s degree in Computer Science from Institut Polytechnique de Paris and was a student of Telecom SudParis Engineeering School.

My research aims to provide better abstractions and software/hardware implementation of systems for processing semi-structured data and streams.
